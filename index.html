About the Horses

Lucky

Lucky is a fun horse, but he has a funny gait. When Lucky wins a race, it's usually because the other horses didn't decide to run out of the gate. Every now and then, he gets lucky.

Bugle

Bugle is an old horse that loves the bugler. Whenever the bugler plays his tune, Bugle becomes very happy. He has been known to even do a dance as the bugle plays.

Stud

Stud was thought to be a male horse, but as it turns out, he's a filly! Male or female, Stud is one of the best horses around. Stud likes to break first and leave the other horses in the mud.

Jeff Lile - Code Louisville - Fall 2020
